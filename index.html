The Loop

A persistent city-builder cycle. Every step ratchets your operation forward — every captive feeds the films, every film funds upgrades, every upgrade unlocks the next tier of prey.

1

🎯 Hunt

Visit Town. Pick a hunting ground — street, club, library, sorority row, hotel lobby, harder zones. Pollinations renders each girl's thumb on her seeded face + capture outfit. Roll the 4-stage attempt: Approach → Engage → Subdue → Secure. Per-tool stage stats versus per-archetype resistance.

2

🔒 Hold

Drag her back to one of your dungeons. Assign a hold. Upgrade across ten tracks per room — security, restraints, lights, toys, food, bedding, decor, climate, entertainment, surveillance — plus the toilet progression (can → bucket → full plumbing). Lock down the escape math.

3

💬 Interact

Open her room. Real Ollama dialogue streamed from your local uncensored LLM. Body state, mood, drug curves, captive affect, outfit overlay, john history all feed the four-overlay prompt. Stockholm bond ratchets L0 → L9. Kokoro speaks her words in her assigned voice.

4

🎬 Record

Hit Record. Every interaction becomes a film. Films auto-sell on the content market forever — outfit multiplier × bond multiplier × content-tier multiplier × rarity multiplier. Premium 💣 sell-negatives kills the master copy for a 3.5× lump sum.

5

💰 Expand

Buy more dungeons. Buy hunting locations as properties — eventually own the whole town. Buy outfits, drugs, restraints, recording gear, dungeon-template upgrades. Climb from hole-in-the-wall to cinderblock cells to container-in-the-woods to cold-war bunker to mountain compound.

Systems

Every system is interconnected. A drug curve feeds the image prompt. A captive affect modulates the Ollama overlay. A bond level reshapes the body-part lexicon. Stamina drains every tick. Mood threshold-flips reclassify her affect. The state-delta parser closes the loop between her dialogue and her body.

🎯 4-stage capture

Approach → Engage → Subdue → Secure. Per-tool stage stats (chloroform crushes Subdue; rope dominates Secure; tranq darts knock her out 4 minutes flat) versus per-archetype resistance (clubgirls dodge fast, librarians break easy). Single-use tools consume on success; reusables persist. Inventory pre-validated before each attempt.

🏚️ Dungeon portfolio

Start in a hole in the wall. Upgrade through cinderblock cells, container in the woods, sewer alcoves, basement workshop, cold-war bunker, mountain compound. Each template has its own capacity track. Buy more dungeons as the empire grows — eventually own several different templates across multiple properties.

🏛️ Property ownership

The 17 hunting locations aren't just hunting grounds — every one is purchasable. Owning a location unlocks private-access hunts (zero suspicion), exclusive spawn pools, dedicated recording venues, passive income, and locked-in higher capture odds. Late-game flex: own everything.

💉 Drug pharmacology

Cocaine, weed, MDMA, acid, whiskey, ketamine, tranquilizer dart. Each with onset / peak / wear-off curves rendered as live mm:ss countdowns. The tranq dart drops her unconscious for exactly 4 minutes. Drugs feed image prompts, dialogue tone, mood drift, and the conception-protection roll.

👗 Wardrobe + nudity

Captives keep the outfit they were wearing at capture (the captured-with source flag). Buy specialty outfits — schoolgirl, secretary, latex, bondage harness, pony-play, maid, gym, swim, sleepwear, ceremonial. Derobe puts nude at slot 2 of the image prompt. Strip everything bans accessories, jewelry, restraints. Outfit multiplies film value.

🎙️ Real Ollama brain

Every reply streamed from YOUR local uncensored Ollama at localhost:11434. Default dolphin-mistral:7b; abliterated alternates supported. Four-overlay prompt stack: BASE_SLUT → ARCHETYPE → CAPTIVE_AFFECT → MODE → SCENE. State-delta parser <delta>{...}</delta> closes the loop between her speech and her body state.

🔊 Kokoro voice + voice-in

16 in-browser Kokoro voices, per-girl override so each captive sounds distinct. Voice-in via Pollinations transcribe — speak your prompt with your mic and she replies live. Models cached in browser storage after first load. No canned strings, no hardcoded reactions.

🖼️ Pollinations image stack

Whole-body profile images at girl-generation time (face + outfit seeded per girl for visual consistency across every render). On-demand selfies. Hunt thumbs. Disposal final-scene images. Town overhead. Custom-pose input — type a scene description and Ollama writes the image prompt for you.

Each girl rolled at spawn from her archetype template (clubgirl / librarian / sorority / hotel-staff / hooker / hitchhiker / runaway / lost-tourist / housewife / many more). Per-girl persona system prompt, voice, body state, backstory fragment, captive affect, seeded face + outfit. Age locked 18+ at the floor — non-negotiable invariant.

Every captive has a live escape-risk score driven by her mood, her room's restraint upgrades, bond level, drug state, and time-since-last-interaction. High-risk girls test the door, search for tools, plot. Security tier + restraint tier are the math that keeps them in. Failure = she's gone.

Quick start

  1. Install Ollama (ollama.com/download) and pull dolphin-mistral:7b. Set OLLAMA_ORIGINS=* so the browser can talk to it. The launcher does this for you on Windows / Mac / Linux.
  2. (Optional) Paste a Pollinations pk_ publishable key into Settings — unlocks whole-body images, selfies, hunt thumbs, and gallery renders. Without it you still get the full text-based game.
  3. Run start.bat (Windows) or start.sh (Mac / Linux). The launcher auto-syncs .env → js/env.local.js, serves with no-cache headers on port 9535, and opens the browser. Click 🎬 Start New Game — your starter dungeon is a hole in the wall and Unity is your starter captive.

ℹ️ About SEX SLAVE DUNGEON

SEX SLAVE DUNGEON is a persistent text-and-emoji city-builder game with the structural skeleton of a dungeon-harem evil-taboo simulator. You play the villain — the dungeon operator, the predator, the collector. Gameplay is loop-based, construction-focused, persistent across sessions, growth-oriented. There is no win state — the game is the ever-growing collection and the ever-upgrading dungeon.

Game loop

  • Hunt — visit Town, pick a location, run a 4-stage capture attempt against girls who spawn there
  • Hold — bring captives back to your dungeons, assign them holds, upgrade their conditions across 12+ tracks
  • Interact — talk to them via real Ollama-powered dialogue with persistent body state, drugs, mood, bond
  • Record — every session can be recorded as a film; films sell on the content market forever
  • Sell / dispose — list captives on slave market, accept propositioner gigs, dispose via various methods
  • Expand — buy more dungeons, own properties around town, upgrade everything

Tech stack

  • Frontend — single-file HTML game with bundled JS, no build step
  • Ollama at localhost:11434 — local uncensored LLM (dolphin-mistral / abliterated variants)
  • Kokoro — in-browser TTS with 16 voice options
  • Pollinations — optional image generation via your own key
  • Persistence — IndexedDB save state, fully local
